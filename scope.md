# Scope

## In Scope
- Online orders, retail Shopify, POS (manual), warranty claims.
- Replacement orders.
- Return label generation.
- Documentation capture.
- Automated refunds (where supported).
- Claimlane ticket creation and customer communication.
- Product categories: mattresses, toppers, furniture, bedding, bath, accessories, weighted blankets, custom.

## Regions
- Canada
- United States

## Channels
- Web (.com/.ca)
- Shopify
- POS
- Third‑party vendor (guidance only)

## Out of Scope
- Full POS automation.
- Amazon/Wayfair integrations.
- Markets outside CA/US.
- Multi‑language beyond English at launch.
- Fraud detection systems.
- Enhanced chatbot functionality.
