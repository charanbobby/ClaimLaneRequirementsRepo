# How to Use This BRD

## Purpose

This Business Requirements Document – Claimlane Returns, Refunds and Warranty Portal – defines how Silk & Snow will replace an ad-hoc email-based returns and warranty process with a guided self-service portal. It describes the portal’s objectives, scope, actors, high-level flows, functional and non-functional requirements, business rules, constraints, dependencies, risks, open questions, epics, user stories and identified gaps.

## Document Structure

- overview-purpose.md – Overall overview of the portal and its objectives.
- scope.md – In-scope and out-of-scope processes, products, regions and channels.
- actors.md – Key actors and their roles.
- process-high-level.md – Narrative of the end-to-end high-level process.
- functional-requirements.md – Detailed functional requirements.
- non-functional-requirements.md – Non-functional requirements.
- business-rules.md – Business rules.
- constraints-assumptions.md – Constraints and assumptions.
- dependencies.md – Dependencies and integrations.
- risks-mitigation.md – Risks and mitigation items.
- open-questions.md – Open questions needing S&S decisions.
- epics-user-stories.md – Epics, features, user stories and acceptance criteria.
- gaps-conflicts.md – Gaps and potential conflicts.
